Honey Glazed Chicken

About the Recipe

A light meal that everyone can enjoy. Sweet and delicious chicken served on a plate of rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chiken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chiken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 mintutes more.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
